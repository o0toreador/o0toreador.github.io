New World

  • New World is a MMORPG developed by Amazon Games. In New World, players explore a thrilling open world filled with danger and opportunity where they forge a new destiny on the supernatural island of Aeternum.

    As an UX designer, I:
    • Work collaboratively with other Designers and partner with design teams across the studio to craft amazing experiences for New World players
    • Partner with UX and game design teams to craft menus, HUD elements, in-world graphic design elements, logos and icons, animations, and motion graphics
    • Develop creative visual solutions to complex game systems and ensure balance between form and function
    • Pitch and present designs to product owners, other designers, and stakeholders

MLB The Show 23

  • MLB The Show 23 was a AAA baseball video game developed by SIE San Diego Studio. It was the 18th title in The Show series.

    Awards:
    Sports Game of the Year - 27th Annual DICE Awards
    Sports Game of the Year - Operation Sports 2023
    Best Sports Game - IGN Awards 2023

    As an UX designer, I:
    • Worked closely with producers, game designers, artists, engineers, and UX research to create wireframes, user-flows, and interfaces
    • Designed logic, layout, and flow of the user interfaces based on information architecture and accessibility principles
    • Conceived transitions and animated effects to enhance gameplay experience
    • Created detailed specifications for UI implementation
    • Managed and prioritized work in order to deliver designs in a timely manner
    • Helped develop best practices, workflows, and design pipelines within the UI team
