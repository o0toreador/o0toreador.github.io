MLB The Show 19

  • MLB The Show 19 is a AAA baseball video game developed by SIE San Diego Studio and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It is the fourteenth entry of the MLB The Show franchise. The game was recently released on March 26, 2019.

    As an interface artist, my primary responsibilities on this title included:
    • Contribute ideas and mockups that have a direct impact on the overall aesthetics of the game
    • Create templates and guidelines to improve the process of future live team assets creation
    • Work closely with game designers and senior artists to design the best experience for our users
    • Attend daily stand-ups and meetings with the front-end team

MLB The Show 18

  • MLB The Show 18 is a AAA baseball video game developed by SIE San Diego Studio and published by Sony Interactive Entertainment. It is the thirteenth entry of the MLB The Show franchise. The game was released on March 27, 2018.

    As an interface artist, my primary responsibilities on this title included:
    • Meet daily deadlines to design hundreds of images that support revenue-generating game content
    • Uphold MLB’s brand and visual identity while using art to communicate information in-game
    • Design graphics for Twitch live streams, social media and the game's official website, TheShowNation.com
    • Attend weekly meetings with the live content team
    • Help train other artist on the design process at PlayStation
