Hi, welcome to my portfolio site! My name is Aslanta Chen, and I'm an undergraduate student at Northeastern University studying Games with a minor in Graphic and Information Design. I will graduate from Northeastern with a BFA degree in May 2020.

At Northeastern University, I'm involved with the Game Development Club, Association of Gaming Enthusiasts, Chinese Students & Scholars Association, NU Culture and Language Learning Society, and NU Ballroom Dance Club. I also serve as a tour guide and a Mandarin peer tutor on campus. Outside of class, I work with Professor Celia Pearce and Brandon Sichling to help develop Who Is R. Mutt? and Beauty of Poppies.

Please leave me a message at aslantachen@gmail.com, I would love to hear from you!
