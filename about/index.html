Hi, welcome to my portfolio site! My name is Aslanta Chen, and I'm currently a student at Northeastern University working towards a BFA in Games with a minor in Graphic and Information Design. I'm an artist, game designer, and project manager.

This summer, I worked at Sony Interactive Entertainment (also known as PlayStation) as an interface artist intern. This experience has greatly improved my art skills and gave me valuable insights on the AAA industry. I'm currently looking for more opportunities to get involved in the industry, possibly another internship at a large-/medium-sized game company.

At Northeastern University, I'm currently involved with the Game Development Club, NU Stage Musical Theater Company, Chinese Students & Scholars Association, NU Culture and Language Learning Society, and Association of Gaming Enthusiasts. I'm also a campus tour guide and a Mandarin peer tutor. Outside of class, I work with Professor Celia Pearce and Professor Brandon Sichling to help produce Who Is R. Mutt? and Beauty of Poppies.
