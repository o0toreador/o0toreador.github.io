DIGITAL GAMES

  • Struggling Dreams


    Role: Artist, Game Designer


    Winner of the Goethe Institut gr8artgames Boston Game Jam (2017). A 2D art game in which the player uses the mouse (click and hold) to help refugees remember and envision better and safer times so they can gather the strength to move on and escape from danger.


    Windows Mac

  • House of Mirrors


    Role: Art Lead


    A 2D puzzle pseudo horror game created for the Global Game Jam (2018). The player must transmit their soul between mediums to find their way through a labyrinth of rooms. Can you find your way out before the House of Mirrors swallows you whole, or will you be able to traverse the maze and find the secrets that lie within?


    Windows

  • Butt Out


    Role: UI Artist


    A 2-player 2D fighting game created for the Northeastern University Game Development Club Game Jam (2017). Select from a roster of wrestling yarn puppets. Run, jump, and try to butt bounce your enemy off the map. Yarn power ups will fall and make your butt bigger and increase the effectiveness of your butt bounce.

  • Who Is R. Mutt? (IN PROGRESS)


    Role: Project Manager, Artist


    An alternate reality game being developed by Paidia Studios under Professor Celia Pearce. It centers around the famous readymade "The Fountain," a piece attributed to Marcel Duchamp. However, the discovery of a letter changed everything.

  • Beauty of Poppies (IN PROGRESS)


    Role: Producer


    The Beauty of Poppies is a story-driven 16-bit RPG being developed under Professor Brandon Sichling. The story happens around “The Corporal,” a Marine stationed in Camp Leatherneck, Afghanistan. His choices decide whether the camp runoff will help Aysiir, who assists a poppy farmer, or Vera, a private military commander guarding a girls’ school.
