Indie Games

  • Struggling Dreams


    Role: Artist, Game Designer


    Winner of the Goethe Institut gr8artgames Boston Game Jam (2017). A 2D art game in which the player uses the mouse (click and hold) to help refugees remember and envision better and safer times so they can gather the strength to move on and escape from danger. The team was sponsored to showcase the game at A MAZE. / Berlin (2018).


    Windows Mac

  • Tastes Like Home


    Role: UI Artist, Logo Artist


    A 2D simulation game created for the Global Game Jam (2019). Welcome to Tastes Like Home, the magical pop-up tea shop. Here we carry everything from classics to the most whimsical blends – whatever fits your palette. Come in, try a sip, and we guarantee that it’ll taste just like home.

  • House of Mirrors


    Role: Art Lead, Game Designer


    A 2D puzzle pseudo horror game created for the Global Game Jam (2018). The player must transmit their soul between mediums to find their way through a labyrinth of rooms. Can you find your way out before the House of Mirrors swallows you whole, or will you be able to traverse the maze and find the secrets that lie within?


    Windows

  • Playground Counselor


    Role: Art Lead, Game Designer


    A text-based 2D puzzle game created for the IGDA San Diego Game Jam (2018). A game where you play as the brilliant Dr. Pako as he helps his kindergarten patient, while Dr. Pako is a kindergartener himself.


    Windows Mac

  • Taco Time


    Role: Artist, Game Designer


    A 2D puzzle game created for Ludum Dare 42 (2018). As the taco chef, the player must grab the correct ingredients to complete the order, while grabbing the wrong ingredients cancels the order. Remember to not touch any dirty socks and fish bones, or your restaurant will be shut down!


    Browser

  • Butt Out


    Role: UI Artist, Sound Designer


    A 2-player 2D fighting game created for the Northeastern University Game Development Club Game Jam (2017). Select from a roster of wrestling yarn puppets. Run, jump, and try to butt bounce your enemy off the map. Yarn power ups will fall and make your butt bigger and increase the effectiveness of your butt bounce.


    Windows

  • Who Is R. Mutt? (IN DEVELOPMENT)


    Role: UI Art Lead, Project Manager


    An alternate reality game being developed by Paidia Studios under Professor Celia Pearce. The game centers around the famous readymade "The Fountain," a piece attributed to Marcel Duchamp. My role on the team is to set up art direction for for the app and the website, produce assets and icons when needed, and schedule and organize weekly meetings with the app developers & web developers.

  • Beauty of Poppies (IN DEVELOPMENT)


    Role: Producer


    The Beauty of Poppies is a story-driven RPG being developed under Professor Brandon Sichling. Players take the role of “The Corporal,” a Marine stationed in Afghanistan whose choices decide what the people in the region can become. My role on the team is to check on other team members' progress weekly, apply for internal and external grants, and determine the project budget and timeline.
